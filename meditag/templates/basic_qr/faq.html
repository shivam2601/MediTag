Emergencies-

Please specify the antidotes and procedure in case of an emergency. Eg., aspirin or any other blood thinner in case of heart attacks, steps to follow in case of a special condition that you’re prone to and might need an immediate response.

Existing conditions-

Genetic diseases like diabetes, bp; cancer, thyroid, diabetes, respiratory diseases like asthma, seizures, chest pain, migraines, panic attacks, syndromes, aids, etc.

Disabilities-

What’s considered a disability is a very sensitive and debatable topic, hence it’s entirely on you to judge the need to mention something in the column.

Others-

If you’re confused about mentioning something in any of the other columns then this is the go-to option for it.
